Quick answers

Common questions

How do I place an order?

You can reach us via WhatsApp, call, email, or by filling the form on this page. Once we confirm your order and location, we'll arrange payment and delivery. We're working on a full online store — coming soon.

Do you deliver outside Lagos?

Yes. We deliver nationwide via third-party couriers. Delivery timelines vary by state — typically 2–5 business days outside Lagos. Delivery costs are calculated at checkout.

What if I'm not happy with my order?

We stand behind everything we make. If something isn't right — reach out within 7 days and we'll replace it or give you a full refund. No runaround, no hassle.

Do you do wholesale or bulk orders?

Absolutely. We work with retailers, event planners, corporate gifting clients, and more. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a custom quote.

Are your products suitable as gifts?

They're perfect for gifting. We offer our Complete Gift set in beautiful packaging, and we can also do custom gift wrapping for corporate or special occasion orders. Just let us know.
