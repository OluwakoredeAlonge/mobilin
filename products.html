Quick answers

What are the 5 Mobiliin snacks?

We currently offer five handcrafted snacks: the Peanut Brittle Bar, Rice Krispies Bar, Oat Bar, Coconut Candy Bar, and Corn Cookies. All are made with 100% natural ingredients here in Lagos.

Are there any artificial ingredients?

Absolutely not. Every Mobiliin snack is made with 100% natural ingredients — no artificial colours, no preservatives, no flavour enhancers. What you see on the label is exactly what's inside.

Can I order multiple different snacks together?

Yes! We love mix-and-match orders. Check out our Combo Pack (any 3 snacks) or the Full Collection Box (all 5). You can also contact us directly to customise your order.

How do I place an order?

WhatsApp us, call us, or fill the contact form on our Contact page. Tell us what you'd like and where to deliver. We'll confirm your order and arrange payment — it's that simple.
