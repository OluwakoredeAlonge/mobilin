How it started

A kitchen in Surulere.
A health-first mission.

It started the way most real things do — not with a business plan, but with a concern. Founder Tolu Adeyemi had spent years watching snack brands quietly fill their products with artificial preservatives, synthetic colours, and cheap ingredients. The taste would change. The nutrition would vanish. The thing that made it good would disappear without a word.

She noticed. And she was tired of it.

In 2019, working out of her mother's kitchen in Surulere, Tolu began making snacks the way she believed they should be made — real ingredients, no preservatives, health first. Coconut candy bars made with real coconut. Oat bars sweetened with honey. Krispies bars packed with clean, simple ingredients. Peanut brittle made the old-fashioned way. Corn cookies from actual corn flour.

She gave them to neighbours, friends, colleagues — anyone willing to give honest feedback. The response was always the same: "Why doesn't the supermarket sell this?"

Growing up

From Surulere
to all 36 states.

Word spread the way it only does when something is genuinely good. By 2021, Mobiliin had outgrown the kitchen and moved into a production space in Yaba. The team grew — carefully, deliberately. Every person hired had to believe the same thing: that healthy eating shouldn't be a luxury, and that real ingredients are non-negotiable.

Today, Mobiliin delivers to all 36 states across Nigeria. From Lagos to Kano, from Abuja to Port Harcourt — our five handcrafted snacks find their way to every corner of the country.

The kitchen in Surulere is gone. The obsession with quality hasn't.

Our Mission

To make healthy snacking accessible, delicious, and available to every Nigerian.

We believe everyone deserves snacks that are genuinely good for them — not just in Lagos, but in every state across Nigeria. Our mission is to prove that healthy food can taste incredible, and that eating well doesn't have to come at a premium.

Our Vision

A Nigerian brand recognised globally for wholesome, honest snacks.

We want Mobiliin to be the name people think of when they think of healthy snacking — not just in Nigeria, but everywhere. Proof that a Lagos brand can stand shoulder-to-shoulder with the best in the world.
