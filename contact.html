Quick answers

Common questions

How do I place an order?

The quickest way is WhatsApp — just message us with your order and location. You can also call, email, or fill the form above. Once confirmed, we arrange payment and dispatch.

Which snacks do you currently sell?

We currently offer five handcrafted snacks: the Peanut Brittle Bar, Rice Krispies Bar, Oat Bar, Coconut Candy Bar, and Corn Cookies. All are priced at ₦1,500 per unit, with bundle discounts available.

Do you deliver outside Lagos?

Yes! We deliver to all 36 states in Nigeria. Lagos orders arrive within 24 hours. Other states receive their snacks within 2–5 business days via our trusted courier partners.

What if I'm not happy with my order?

We stand behind everything we make. If something isn't right, reach out within 7 days and we'll replace it or issue a full refund. No runaround, no stress.

Do you do wholesale or bulk orders?

Absolutely. We work with retailers, corporate clients, event planners, and more. Contact us with your requirements and we'll put together a competitive custom quote.

Are the snacks suitable as gifts?

They're perfect for gifting. Our Full Collection Box features all five snacks in beautiful packaging — a thoughtful, health-conscious gift. We also do custom wrapping for corporate or special occasion orders.
